Total fertility rate (live births per woman)

UN data: United Nations Population Division

The 2019 Revision of World Population Prospects represents the latest global set of demographic estimates and projections prepared by the Population Division of the Department of Economic and Social Affairs of the United Nations Secretariat. It displays key demographic indicators for selected periods for the world, development groups, regions, subregions, and countries or areas with more than 90,000 inhabitants in 2019.
